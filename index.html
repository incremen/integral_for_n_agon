The Integral n-gon

Graph this function from $X = 0$ to $X = n$ and you get a regular n-gon.

$$z(X) = \int_0^X e^{i \frac{2\pi}{n} \sum_{k=1}^n \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(x-k)\theta}}}\,dx$$
